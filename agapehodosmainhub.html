Agape Hodos

What is Agape Hodos?

We envision campuses alive and active for Jesus. Christians united across organizations to meet community needs simply because they love Christ. Agape Hodos is designed to work alongside other ministries and spread organically as its impact is observed.

What does Agape Hodos mean?

Agape Hodos translates to "The Way of God's Love." Jesus is the Way, and we seek to love as He loved--actively, sacrificially, and publicaly. We exist to connect the Church to the community, not replace discipleship ministries or local churches.
